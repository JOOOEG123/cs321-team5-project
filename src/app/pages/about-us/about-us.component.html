About Us

We are a team of four Computer Science students at George Mason University, and this is our application for our Software Engineering Course (CS321). This application is the brainchild of Seth Harding for our Software Engineering course, which we were tasked to build as a team. Being a dungeon master, he wanted an application which could be used to simplify the role of notekeeping for a campaign. Working together as a team, we were able to create an application for location-based notekeeping we could call our own.

FAQ

What does this cost? Nothing it is absolutely free.

Can I use this from games other than DND? Of course, this is designed to be system agnostic so any tabletop game can work.

Can I use this to organize offical Modules? Yes but only for modules you already own.
